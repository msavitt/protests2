Most Attended Protests in U.S. History

Protests that call for progressive change in America have continued, and grown, in the last 20 years. (Michayla Savitt/WRFI)

Here are five of the country's protests with top attendance:

  1. Demonstrations against police brutality following George Floyd's murder drew in about 26 million protestors nataionwide.
  2. The Women's March in January 2017 had about 5.6 million attendees across the country.
  3. March for Our Lives, a student-led protest in 2018 against gun violence, drew about 2 million people
  4. The Women's March in January 2018 reportedly had about 1.5 million attendees.
  5. March on Washington for Lesbian, Gay, and Bisexual Equal Rights and Liberation in 1993 had about 1 million people in attendance.
